CoderDojo Tokyo

CoderDojo, an opensource programming club for kids

CoderDojo Tokyo opens every Sunday at Shimokitazawa OpenSource Cafe, and teaches kids how to

  • do programming with Scratch
  • build your own website with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • make things with Arduino
